WolfieNet is Stony Brook University's Wi-Fi (wireless) network on West Campus, Southampton, Manhattan, and certain parts of East Campus. It is available to all faculty, students, staff, and guests. New locations continue to be added and wireless technology and equipment continues to be upgraded and enhanced in order to handle the growing number of devices seeking to connect to the network.
